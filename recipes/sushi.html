Sushi

Description

A simple homemade sushi recipe

Ingredients

  • rice (rice needs to sticky to each other so it is better to use sushi rice)
  • Vinegar(better rice vinegar)
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Mirin or sweet sake
  • Fish fillet or any filling you like
  • Nori sheets
  • Sushi mat

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice. Then cook it for about 20 minutes.
  2. While the rice is cooking, put about 200ml of vinegar at a pan, sugar at taste, a pinch of salt, 2 table spoon of mirin or sake, and heat until sugar melts and the vinegar evaporets for some time without letting it boil(more or less sour)
  3. After the rice is coocked and the vinegar sauce is ready, put them at a recipient and mix them until the rice is hydrated (not too wet)
  4. Cut the fish in stripes as long as nori sheets
  5. Put the nori sheets on the sushi mat with the wrinkled side up
  6. After the rice had cooled down, get a small portion, enough to fill the nori sheet with a layer of rice, leaving about 1 cm rice-free at top
  7. Put the sliced fish(or any other filling) in the middle, and wrap using the sushi mat.
  8. Seal the sushi with some water on the rice-free top side
