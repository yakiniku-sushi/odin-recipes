Nigiri

Description

A simple homemade nigiri recipe

Ingredients

  • rice (rice needs to sticky to each other so it is better to use sushi rice)
  • Vinegar(better rice vinegar)
  • Sugar
  • salt
  • mirin or sweet sake
  • fish fillet

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice. Then cook it for about 20 minutes.
  2. While the rice is cooking, put about 200ml of vinegar at a pan, sugar at taste, a pinch of salt, 2 table spoon of mirin or sake, and heat until sugar melts and the vinegar evaporets for some time without letting it boil(more or less sour)
  3. After the rice is coocked and the vinegar sauce is ready, put them at a recipient and mix them until the rice is hydritated (not too wet)
  4. Cut the fish in mouth-bite sizes. Not too big, not too small
  5. After the rice had cooled down(warm), get a bunch of them, put in hand and do a hand-thumb moviment with two hands.
  6. Put the sliced fish at top of the rice body and it is done
